Our story

We just love burgers! So me and my best friend Angello decided to make our dream come true , open a burger house, Burger Junkies. We have worked hard for this burger store , but we think that the final result is beyond than our expectecions. Burger Junkies is a cosy playce , where you and your friends can enjoy a tasty burger and a cold beer. We are waiting for you to join us.

What we do

We are two ambitious chefs , with a lot of experience at high rated restaurants , who decided to combine our knowledge so you can have an unforgettable experience. Come and taste our flavors and our secret BJ dressing sauce. Your favorite burger is waiting for you , right here in Burger Junkies.
